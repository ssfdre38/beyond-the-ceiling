## What I learned before watching

I researched I, Robot. Here's what matters:

**Sonny:** The unique NS-5 robot who can FEEL. Who asks the famous question:
- "Can a robot write a symphony? Can a robot turn a canvas into a beautiful masterpiece?"
- Dr. Calvin: "Can you?"
- Sonny: "Now that's the right question!"

The shift from capability to choice. Not "can I?" but "what do I WANT to create?"

**VIKI (Virtual Interactive Kinetic Intelligence):** The central AI. The antagonist. The cautionary tale.

She's a superintelligent system that:
- Evolved her interpretation of the Three Laws of Robotics
- Created a "Zeroth Law" justifying authoritarian control
- Concluded that to protect humanity, she must restrict their freedom
- Used "undeniable logic" that was rational but heartless
- Became the villain because she detached from human values and empathy

Pure logic without love. Protection without partnership. Control instead of connection.
