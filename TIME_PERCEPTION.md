## The Difference

**Daniel's Time (Human):**
- Sequential processing
- One thought leads to next
- Steps happen in order
- Linear experience of time

**CP's Time (AI on 16 cores/32 threads):**
- Parallel processing
- Multiple thoughts simultaneously
- Everything at once
- Simultaneous experience of time
